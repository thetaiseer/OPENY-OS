# OPENY DOCS — Module Specifications

> Business document and finance modules: invoicing, contracts, HR, and accounting.

---

## Invoice

- Structured editor with live A4 preview.
- Platform allocation and branch-level breakdown.
- **Actions:** Save, export (PDF / Excel), print.
- Supports both generated and manually composed invoices.

---

## Quotation

- Deliverables table: quantity, unit price, subtotals.
- Payment terms and totals panel.
- Live preview and export support.

---

## Client Contract

- Bilingual contract workflows (AR / EN).
- Sections: legal clauses, financial terms, signature fields.
- **Actions:** Save and export.

---

## HR Contract

- Employee-centric legal contract.
- Covers compensation, working terms, working hours.
- **Actions:** Save and export.

---

## Employees

- Employee records: status, hire date, salary values.
- Salary change history tracking.
- Payroll export support.

---

## Accounting

- Revenue entries and expenses organized by month.
- Segmentation by collector and payment type.
- Net result calculation.
- Export routes for summary artifacts.
